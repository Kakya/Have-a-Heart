Have a Heart

  • High Concept: Branching dialogue story game.
  • Player Experience Goals: To have fun interacting with the story through dialogue choices.
  • Genre: The game is a text adventure.
  • Game play: The player selects from the list of dialogue options, which all lead to various outcomes. The choices are denoted, but are mostly either W or S to pick.
  • Prototype goal: Work with branching dialogue.
  • Features: Branching dialogue and available in many languages.
  • Setting/Premise: You are in an unfamiliar locale, looks like a hospital. You will uncover more and more from the story as you stay longer.
  • Story: The tension will entirely come from the dramatic tension inside the story, once more of the story is complete.
  • Target Audience: All those who like text-based games focused on dialogue.
  • Play time: As long as it takes to go through the dialogue.
  • Strategies: There won't really be strategy as the game is more about the dialogue.
